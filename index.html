About Jory Catering

Jory Catering Services is a leading UAE-based school catering company committed to providing high-quality, nutritious meals to students across Abu Dhabi, Al Ain, and Dubai.

We understand that proper nutrition plays a vital role in students' performance and well-being. Our team of experienced chefs and nutritionists work together to create balanced menus that meet international food safety standards including ADAFSA and HACCP compliance.
